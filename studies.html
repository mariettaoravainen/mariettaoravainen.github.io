Studies

In my first year of studies, I had the opportunity to engage in two significant group projects. The first project took place in the fall of 2022, where our team worked on creating a physical, functional tic-tac-toe game using Arduino. This involved both the construction of the game using Arduino hardware and the coding of the necessary program using the Arduino IDE.

In the spring of 2023, our focus shifted entirely towards coding in a project centered around simulating a banking system. This project provided a deep dive into the intricacies of programming. Towards the end of the first academic year, a crucial decision point arrived: the choice between specializing in software or hardware. I opted for the software track, aligning my academic path with a focus on software development.

The structure of the second year of my studies mirrors that of the first, with one project in the fall and another in the spring. These projects are typically collaborative efforts, allowing for extensive experience in teamwork and collaboration.

The project for the fall of 2023 during my second year of studies was centered around creating a movie website. As a group, our task was to develop a functional website dedicated to movies. This involved enabling users to create accounts, browse through a collection of films, and write reviews for the movies. Additionally, we created a promotional video for the website and uploaded it to YouTube.

In spring 2024, I participated in the Joffer project, a collaboration with Fontys University in the Netherlands. Our team developed the mobile version of the Joffer job search platform, working alongside OAMK students handling the web version and Fontys students managing the backend. This experience enhanced my teamwork skills and my understanding of collaborative software development.
